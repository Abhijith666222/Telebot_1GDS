📝 *Eligibility*

*New Injury during NS*  
> No prior history of any related injuries/conditions. *(Determined by MO)*

*Pre-existing Condition*  
> If aggravated or clinical worsening due to service. *(Determined by MO)*

*Coverage*  
> Fully subsidised medical benefits for as long as required.

*Service Injury Card*  
> Issued for servicemen who require medical treatment for more than 6 weeks.  
> Approval required to attribute injury to service.
